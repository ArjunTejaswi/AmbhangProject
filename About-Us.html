Who Are We?

Ambang Jaya Engineering & Trading Sdn Bhd (AJETSB) is an oil and gas solution provider based in Malaysia since 2005. We serve a wide spectrum of essential sectors in Malaysia such as oil & gas, petrochemicals, manufacturing, power plants and utilities. AJETSB is transforming itself into a corporate company that deepens its existing business and explores into new fields simultaneously. We continue to tackle the tasks that are necessary to promote the creation of more sustainable world in terms of the environments as well as enabling social and economics progress.
